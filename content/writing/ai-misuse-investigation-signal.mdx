AI misuse investigation can become noisy because the same event may contain automation, human intent, commodity tooling, and normal platform behavior.

The useful first move is boundary setting: what telemetry exists, what hypothesis is being tested, what would falsify it, and what evidence is not available. That structure keeps the investigation from turning into a story assembled around an early suspicion.
