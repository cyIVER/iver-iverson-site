Autonomous triage can accelerate evidence gathering, but the verdict layer should remain deterministic. A clear ladder for true positive, benign positive, false positive, and requires-investigation prevents language fluency from masquerading as confidence.

The triage brief should show what was queried, what was observed, which rule fired, and what uncertainty remains.
