## Problem

Advanced cyber defense training needs a controlled place to connect forensics, malware reverse engineering, telemetry, and hunting. DCO 550 structures that capstone environment around isolated infrastructure and mission briefings.

## Constraints

- Malware-analysis environments needed isolation by design.
- Golden images and telemetry pipelines had to be reproducible.
- Public portfolio treatment needed to describe architecture without operationalizing sensitive techniques.

## Approach

DCO 550 uses an Astro-based mission briefing platform, Packer-built analysis images, Azure cyber range infrastructure, and telemetry streaming into Microsoft Defender for Endpoint and Sentinel.

## Artifact And Proof

The supporting artifact summarizes the course platform, range lifecycle, image build flow, and telemetry integration boundaries.

## Outcomes And Status

The platform extends the portfolio from investigation tooling into advanced training infrastructure: students work in environments where adversary behavior, forensic evidence, and telemetry can be reasoned about together.
