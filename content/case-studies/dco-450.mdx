## Problem

Cyber hunt instruction needs more than slides. Students need mission context, repeatable ranges, telemetry, and briefing material that connects actor behavior to practical hunting.

## Constraints

- Course delivery and range infrastructure had separate lifecycle needs.
- Azure range automation needed reproducible build paths and clear environment boundaries.
- Public description had to avoid presenting sensitive curriculum internals as open operational guidance.

## Approach

DCO 450 combines an Astro mission platform with Terraform and Packer automation for range infrastructure. The platform supports actor analysis, intelligence engineering, behavioral analytics, lab documentation, and Sentinel-backed telemetry workflows.

## Artifact And Proof

The supporting artifact captures the dual-track CI/CD model: GitHub Actions for the mission platform and GitLab CI/CD for Azure range infrastructure.

## Outcomes And Status

DCO 450 shows the education side of the same investigation thesis: create realistic, instrumented environments where hunting concepts become observable work.
